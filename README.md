# 🌾 AgriConnect

AgriConnect is a web-based platform designed to connect **local farmers** directly with **consumers and small businesses**. The system aims to eliminate middlemen, promote fair trade, and increase farmers’ income by providing a simple and efficient online marketplace for fresh produce.

With AgriConnect, farmers can easily **list their products**, **manage orders**, and **track sales**, while consumers can **browse**, **purchase**, and **communicate** directly with farmers. The project supports **sustainable agriculture**, **local community empowerment**, and **digital transformation** in the agricultural sector.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 👨‍🌾 Farmer Side
- **Farmer Registration & Login** – Farmers can create and manage their accounts.  
- **Product Management** – Add, edit, and delete products with images, prices, and descriptions.  
- **Order Management** – View, confirm, and track customer orders.  
- **Sales Dashboard** – Track total sales, top products, and inventory levels.  

### 🛒 Consumer Side
- **Customer Registration & Login** – Buyers can create accounts and log in securely.  
- **Product Browsing & Search** – Explore products by category, price, or location.  
- **Shopping Cart & Checkout** – Add items to cart and place orders.  
- **Order Tracking** – Monitor order status (Pending, Accepted, Completed).  

### 🧩 Admin Side
- **User Management** – Manage farmer and consumer accounts.  
- **Product Monitoring** – Oversee all listed items for quality control.  
- **Report Generation** – Generate reports for users, sales, and popular products.  
- **Announcements & Notifications** – Post updates about agricultural programs and system news.  

### 💬 Additional Features (Optional)
- **Messaging System** – Direct communication between farmers and buyers.  
- **Feedback & Ratings** – Buyers can rate products or sellers.  
- **Responsive Design** – Works smoothly on mobile and desktop devices.  
- **Location-Based Filtering** – Buyers can find nearby farmers or delivery options.
